Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our research compounds and services

What is the purity of your peptides?

All our peptides are manufactured to achieve ≥99% purity. Each batch undergoes rigorous third-party testing including HPLC analysis and mass spectrometry to verify purity and identity. We provide a Certificate of Analysis (COA) with every product.

Are these products for human consumption?

No, all our products are strictly for research purposes only. They are not intended for human consumption, medical use, or any therapeutic applications. These compounds are designed for qualified researchers and institutions conducting legitimate scientific studies.

How should I store the peptides?

Lyophilized peptides should be stored at -20°C in a dry environment. Once reconstituted, store at 2-8°C and use within the timeframe specified on the product documentation. Always handle with appropriate laboratory safety measures.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer fast and secure shipping worldwide. Most orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Shipping times vary by location: 2-3 days domestic, 5-10 days international. All products are shipped with appropriate temperature control and discrete packaging.

Do you provide Certificates of Analysis?

Yes, every product comes with a comprehensive Certificate of Analysis (COA) that includes HPLC chromatography, mass spectrometry results, purity verification, and batch-specific quality control data. COAs are available upon request or with your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways. Institutional customers may also arrange for purchase orders and NET payment terms.

Can I return or exchange products?

Due to the nature of research compounds, we cannot accept returns of opened products. However, if you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We stand behind our quality and will resolve any legitimate issues promptly.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer competitive bulk pricing for qualified research institutions and regular customers. Volume discounts are available starting at 10+ units. Contact our sales team for custom quotes and institutional pricing arrangements.
